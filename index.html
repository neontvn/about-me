Philosophy

How I think

Engineering principles that guide every system I design.

01

Performance is a feature

Every millisecond matters. I profile before I optimize, and I optimize before I ship.

02

Observe everything

If you can't measure it, you can't fix it. Metrics, traces, and logs are first-class citizens.

03

Design for failure

Systems fail. Good systems fail gracefully. I build with retries, circuit breakers, and graceful degradation.

04

Scale horizontally

Vertical scaling is a dead end. I design stateless services, partitioned data, and elastic infrastructure.

05

Clean abstractions

Good interfaces hide complexity. I write code that is simple to use and hard to misuse.

06

Ship with conviction

I balance engineering rigor with product velocity. The best system is the one that exists.
